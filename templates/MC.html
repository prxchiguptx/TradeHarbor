MARKET CAP

Market Cap" stands for Market Capitalization. Market Cap is calculated by multiplying the current market price (CMP) of a stock by the total number of shares that the company has issued. It tells us the total value of the company in the stock market.For example, if a company's CMP is Rs. 100, and it has 1,000 shares in total, then the Market Cap would be Rs. 100 * 1,000 = Rs. 1,00,000. This means the company is valued at Rs. 1,00,000 in the stock market. So, Market Cap helps investors understand the size of a company in the stock market.
