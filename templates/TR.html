TOTAL REVENUE

"Total Revenue" is like the total amount of money a company earns from selling its products or services. When we talk about a company's total revenue, we're referring to all the money the company earns from selling its products or services during a specific period, such as a year or a quarter. This includes revenue from selling goods, providing services, or any other sources of income the company may have. Total revenue is a key metric for investors because it helps them understand how well a company is performing in terms of generating sales and growing its business. Higher total revenue generally indicates that a company's products or services are in demand and that its business operations are healthy.
